Safety Case Template

It's a template, Please edit the Diagram manually


Details:
  1. By selecting a geometric figure you could edit it.
  2. By clicking ▲ to collapse the diagram.
  3. By clicking ▼ to expand the diagram.
  4. By clicking + to add an new figure below.
  5. Right click to add or delete a figure
  6. By clicking Enter to send the changes which has been made.
  7. By clicking Update data, so you won't lose the changes.
  8. Adejest where those figures are located by Dragging it
  9. Press Zoom to Fit or Center on root, to includes the whole diagram in image.
  10. Please click Download to download the image
